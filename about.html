Rajini [Last Name]

Special Educator & Behavior Analyst

Rajini is a dedicated professional with over 15 years of experience in autism intervention and special education. Her approach combines clinical excellence with deep empathy, understanding that behind every challenging behaviour is a child trying to communicate.

After witnessing the struggles of parents who felt helpless in the face of their children's diagnoses, Rajini founded Sashakt with a singular mission: to train not just children, but to empower families with the skills to support their child's growth for life.

"We don't just 'fix' problems here," Rajini says. "We build bridges. We build the bridge between a child's inner world and the outside world, and we teach parents how to walk that bridge with confidence."
