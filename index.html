The 30-Day Residential Experience

Your child (and you) will live in our specially designed, structured, low-arousal environment for 30 days. Every aspect is designed to reduce anxiety and maximize learning.

Targeting Challenging Behaviours

We reduce aggression and self-injury by understanding the "why" behind the behaviour using the ABC Model.

Social & Emotional Skills

Improve eye contact and conversation through Social Stories and structured practice.

Sensory Processing

Address over-sensitivity and sensation-seeking through personalized Sensory Diets.

Daily Living Independence

Master toileting, dressing, eating, and hygiene through Task Analysis.

Your Child's Personalized Roadmap (IPP)

Every child in our programme receives an Individualized Program Plan (IPP)‚Äîa detailed, flexible document that becomes the roadmap for your child's learning journey.

What Your IPP Includes:

  • Prioritized Goals: Specific targets like "Independently use the toilet within 30 days".
  • Step-by-Step Objectives: Intermediate steps to reach the final goal.
  • Specific Strategies: Exact modifications and teaching methods.
  • Progress Tracking: How we measure success daily.
  • Team Responsibility: Clarity on parent vs. therapist roles.

"The IPP is developed collaboratively with you, reviewed weekly, and adjusted based on your child's progress. You're not just told what we're doing‚Äîyou're part of the team."
